## Not a workflow tool
Positioning

This is not a workflow tool.

underchat co-pilot doesn’t queue tickets or move cards across a board. It risk-grades deals. You interact with it the way you’d interact with a senior analyst: you ask, you direct, you approve.

Workflow SaaS

  • Configure stages, queues, and pipelines
  • Drag deals across boards to make progress
  • Custom forms for the data the team has to fill out
  • Reports built from the fields the team remembered to enter
  • Becomes a place to record decisions, not a tool that makes them

underchat co-pilot

  • Ask the co-pilot a question, get a scored answer with evidence
  • The co-pilot ingests data and grades risk — you don’t fill forms
  • Direct attention: “look into this,” “rescore the book,” “explain this drop”
  • Reports are surfaced on demand, with the underlying reasoning attached
  • Makes decisions easier; doesn’t replace your judgement

If you’re wondering “then how do I use it?” — you talk to it. From the chat surface in your team’s tools, by email, in Slack, or over SMS. The co-pilot reads your deal book, answers questions about it, scores positions, drafts RFIs, and orchestrates actions. You approve.

## Hybrid scoring
The scoring engine

Hybrid ML + agentic scoring. Risk-grade with superior accuracy.

A statistical baseline trained on your wins and losses provides the quantitative floor. An agentic reasoning layer interrogates the structured and unstructured evidence and adjusts the score with cited reasoning. One grade per deal, every component traceable.

1 · ML baselinestatistical

Gradient-boosted model trained on your closed deals, renewals, and unwinds. Captures base-rate patterns across cashflow, deposit behavior, industry, geography, and capital structure.

composes with
2 · Agentic reasoningsituational

Research-grade agent interrogates the evidence the model can’t see: bank letter wording, AR aging quality, communications, RFI responses, missing documents. Adjusts components with cited reasoning.

produces
3 · Institutional scorecarddefensible

One grade per deal. Every component, every assumption, every source document linked. Defensible to your IC, your capital partners, and your auditors.

Δ

ML catches base-rate patterns humans skip. Agentic reasoning catches situational nuance that wrecks purely statistical models. Together: superior accuracy.

## Workflow
How the co-pilot thinks

From raw signal to institutional scorecard.

Five stages run end-to-end on every deal — with humans approving anything that leaves the platform.

01

Ingest

Multimodal data from Plaid, bank statements, QuickBooks, AR/AP, cashflow, bank letters, and RFI responses — structured and unstructured.

02

Score

Hybrid ML and agentic scoring produces an institutional-grade risk grade with cited evidence.

03

Decide

The co-pilot recommends; you approve. Screening, autodecline, offers, renewals, and recovery routing.

04

Orchestrate

RFIs, approvals, and updates flow through the channels your team uses. Strict compliance guardrails. Every action logged.

05

Observe

Portfolio analytics, custom reports, and agent-surfaced insights — on demand.
